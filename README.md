# Credit Card Churn Analysis

**Industry:** BFSI / Finance  
**Tools:** MySQL · Advanced Excel  
**Dataset:** BankChurners.csv (Kaggle — 10,127 records)

---

## Project Objective
To analyse credit card customer data and identify the key drivers of churn, segment at-risk customers, and provide actionable business recommendations to reduce attrition.

---

## Key Findings
- Overall churn rate was 16.07% across 10,127 customers
- Inactive customers with low transaction frequency were 3x more likely to churn
- Customers in the bottom credit limit quartile showed the highest churn rate
- Female customers churned at a slightly higher rate than male customers
- Early warning signs were visible as early as 3 months before confirmed churn
